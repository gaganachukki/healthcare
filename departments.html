Department FAQs

Common questions about visiting our specialized departments.

Do I need a referral to visit a specialist?
No, you can book a direct consultation with most of our specialists without a referral. However, your insurance provider may require one for coverage purposes.
Are the diagnostic labs open 24/7?
Yes, our Emergency diagnostic labs are operational 24/7. Regular out-patient lab services operate from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily.
How can I access my medical records?
You can easily access all your prescriptions, lab reports, and imaging results securely via the Patient Dashboard on this website.
